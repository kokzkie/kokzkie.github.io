Healthy Living Tips

By Nurse Dominic

Top 10 Daily Health Tips

  • 💧 Stay hydrated – aim for at least 8 cups of water daily.
  • 🥗 Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
  • 🚶 Move your body – even a 30-minute walk makes a difference.
  • 😴 Prioritize sleep – adults need 7–9 hours per night.
  • 🧘‍♀️ Practice stress management (deep breathing, meditation, journaling).
  • 🧴 Wash your hands regularly to prevent illness.
  • 🩺 Keep up with regular checkups and screenings.
  • 🚭 Avoid smoking and limit alcohol intake.
  • 🌞 Get some sunlight (Vitamin D!) but wear sunscreen.
  • 😊 Be kind to yourself – mental health is just as important as physical health.
